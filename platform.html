KINO is a single substrate that perceives your enterprise, reasons over its objectives, acts through its systems, and governs every step. Six layers, one coherent architecture, zero glue code.

PERCEIVE

KINO ingests from the live web, your source control, your data warehouses, and your internal systems of record simultaneously. The platform maintains a continuously reconciled model of reality across 190 countries.

Streaming connectors, change data capture, and a native web perception layer mean the agent fleet never operates on stale information.

  • W-01 Native web search & full-page render ingestion
  • W-02 14,000+ prebuilt system connectors
  • W-03 Sub-second change data capture
  • W-04 Continuous reconciliation & drift detection
REASON

A reasoning fabric decomposes any objective into verifiable plans. Deterministic execution paths where safety demands it; adaptive planning where the world is uncertain.

Every plan is checkpointed, explainable, and reproducible. The model of "why" travels with the work.

  • R-01 Objective → plan decomposition engine
  • R-02 Constraint & policy-aware search
  • R-03 Self-correcting adaptive planning
  • R-04 Fully explainable decision traces
ACT

Agents write and ship code, call any REST API, generate documents and speech, and operate your infrastructure. Actions are signed and replayable, never opaque.

KINO does not ask you to copy its output into a terminal. It runs in the terminal.

  • A-01 Code generation, review & auto-merge
  • A-02 Arbitrary HTTP / API orchestration
  • A-03 Document & PDF generation at scale
  • A-04 Sandboxed shell & compute execution
